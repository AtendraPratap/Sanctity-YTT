Founder & CEO

Anil has worked both in India & overseas at CXOs level with companies like True Credits, True Balance, Happy Loans, Net1, Gemalto-Thales, NIIT to name a few. On the lighter side, his idea is simple – Work hard & Party Harder.

Shreyansh Rana
Co-Founder & COO

Shreyansh is passionate about the causes of the bottom of the pyramid “under-resourced” Indian population, where he spent most of his time conceptualizing last mile solutions for them, thus aware of the ground realities, and understands the intricacies involved in these efforts. He brings in over a decade of experience in creating the “Last Mile Distribution Network, Sales Channels and Operations domain” in a diverse set of industries, Ex:Agriculture, FMCG, BFSI, with global giants such as “Cargill, ADM, ETG, Haldiram, Pepsico , Parley” etc to name a few.
