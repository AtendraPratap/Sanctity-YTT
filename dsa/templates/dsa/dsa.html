MoneyPly Direct Service Agents

The job of a DSA is to find potential customers for the bank they represent. To do this, DSAs will look for people who are in the market for a loan. These leads are then directed to the concerned bank or NBFC and the loan process is taken forward. For the effort put in by the DSA, a payout is provided.
